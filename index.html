WHY
ALIBAUG ?

Vast beaches, lavish villas and a lush countryside await you at one of India's fastest growing eco-tourism zones. Simply 15 minutes away from the Gateway of India, Alibaug is closer than you think. It is the closest de-stressing zone to the highest stress city in the country. Despite Alibaug's close proximity to Mumbai, it sustains its tranquility and harmony with nature.
Increasingly recognized as the "Hamptons of India," it is poised to become a first home choice to the creme de la creme of Bombay, given the rapid development in the area. With the approaching construction of a sea-link from Bombay to Alibaug, which will shorten the drive to 1 hour, Alibaug is likely to become an extension of South Bombay. Stylish, state of the art, spacious homes, afford you a serene, and ample lifestyle, much sought after by India's most elite.

SANJAY SEKSARIA
FOUNDATION

Building community awareness, encouraging sustainability and stimulating development. The Sanjay Seksaria Foundation has taken several social initiatives for the betterment of society and the environment in the Alibaug region. The foundation has organized eye camps in the region as well as provided drinking water facilities, in an attempt to make sanitation and healthcare more accessible to the community.
We have also funded infrastructural development by providing streetlights throughout the region. Giving back to nature in the form of planting trees has been one of the biggest and most rewarding initiatives of the foundation.
The SSF invites like-minded individuals or companies, who would like to join us in giving back to the society and are interested in bringing about change in Alibaug.
